🎙️ Voice‑Over Artist

This interview will be recorded. Do you consent to proceed, and please introduce yourself.


Q2 — Voice Experience

Q3 — Performance Sample

Please read the following short script clearly and naturally: “At WestAtlantic, we believe communication should be clear, confident, and human.”
